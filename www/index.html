Introduction
#SpaneWork is a job seeking platform keeping you up-to-date with the latest in local and international jobs. It is an android based platform running on Android 2.3 Gingerbread & above but for better performance Android 4.1 KitKat and above platforms are advised.

Features
1. View posts
2. View posts details
3. Share posts
4. Share app link
5. View calendar
6. App documentation

Terms of Use
Please note this is a post viewing platform and not a job application platform. No curriculum vitae will be received by our group and no form of meetings are coordinated or arranged by our group. It is our users responsibility to validate any and all job posts on the platform. Should any suspision arise on a particular job post please contact helpdesk@spaneapp.com
